How we plan an event?

As our main objective is to enhance the skill of our student members and associate, we do take their suggestions and after making a majority, it is forwarded to the core group of the organization for further brainstorming and approval, once approved we are all set for the event.

Why to join us?

We as an organization believe in your talent, invest in it, we let you experience everything first hand because we believe that problems at hand bring out the best in you, we pay scrupulous attention to the process and are obsessed with accuracy to achieve unmatchable success, so come join us and let's sail together in this voyage of hoving your skill and being the best version of you.
